FAQ

Is my data safe?

Yes. All processing happens in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

Why are some videos missing?

Ads and videos without channel names are filtered out. Only actual content with identifiable channels is counted.

Why don't the numbers match YouTube's count?

Google Takeout only includes data from when watch history was enabled. Private/incognito views aren't tracked.

Can I filter Shorts separately from regular videos?

Unfortunately, Google Takeout doesn't provide any way to distinguish between Shorts and regular videos in the watch history data, so they're counted together.

Does this work on mobile?

Yes, but charts are easier to read on larger screens.
